Dogs Become Valuable Employees At Bank>

It was revealed this morning that a major high street has begun employing clever canines to perform basic data-entry tasks in several of its Uk data centres. In a statement earlier today a spokesperson for the bank said that training had gone well with first intake of ten pooches and that overall they were just happy to be involved.
