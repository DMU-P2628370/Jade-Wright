Zendaya

About

Zendaya played Anna Wheeler in "The Greatest Showman" who performs as one of P.T Barnum's circus acts. She is an introveted, audacious and self-concious African American woman. She is a confident and experienced trapeze artist, having performed with her broth for many years. Throughout the movie Anne is seen to be deeply affected by the racist times of the 19th century.

Zendaya is is an American actress and singer. She began her career as a child model and backup dancer, before gaining prominence for her role as Rocky Blue on the Disney Channel sitcom Shake It Up.

Zac Efron

About

Zac Efron plays Philip Carlyle in "The Greatest Showman", a bright 20 year old playwrite from a prestigous family, who becomes P.T Barnum's partner for the circus. Before joining the circus with Barnum, all he would do was drink, and work on his plays. After joing the circus he became a lot more happy and stopped drinking, he also began to fall in love with Anne Wheeler.

Zac Efron is an American and singer. He Began his acting career Professionally in the early 2000's for his leading role as Troy Bolton in the High School Musical Trilogy. During this time he also starred in the musical film Hairspray and the comedy film 17 Again.
